About this app
The MNP OwnerShip Database 2024 application stands as a thorough solution for retrieving detailed SIM information across all significant Pakistani mobile service providers, including Jazz, Telenor, Ufone, Zong, and Warid. Inputting a mobile number without the leading zero enables users to quickly access important details like the owner's CNIC, name, and address. Furthermore, inputting a CNIC number without dashes reveals the owner's full details, every SIM number registered under that CNIC, along with the corresponding name and address!
